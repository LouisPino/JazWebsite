    Composition Multimedia Percussion Video Art

Jaz Tsui is a Chinese-Canadian interdisciplinary artist, specializing in percussion performance and contemporary improvisation. Their artistic output usually involves bright colors, noise, theatrics, electronic processing, and a hint of silliness. When possible, she loves participating in multimedia projects, either making art or bonking instruments. Jaz has recently finished her Masters degree in Percussion Performance under the tutelage of Aiyun Huang, Beverley Johnston, and Charles Settle. They are currently in the process of figuring out what sort of visual eyesores and auditory experiences they can create next.
